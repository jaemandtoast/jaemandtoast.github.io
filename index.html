About Me

First impressions last, which is why I always strive to make mine phenomenal.

I'm a fourth year who has the wonderful privilege of pursuing a BA in Ethnic Studies at UC Berkeley. Though I fell in love with the major from the first class I took, I had endless concerns about my future financial stability and career pathways. I ultimately decided to pursue it because I realized its powerful potential to contribute to the bigger picture on a global scale. I'm especially passionate about the intersection of marginalized communties, health care, and law/public policy. I'm also intrigued by the power of technology to bring people together and its potential to mobilize certain agendas. I am hoping to take on a position that addresses at least one of these aspects. My life aspiration is to utilize my knowledge in Ethnic Studies to help mobilize nonprofit organizations and start my own nonprofit as well someday.

Cool Stuff

Ch-ch-check out some cool stuff that I was involved with.

HackingEDU

HackingEDU is a hackathon conceived and executed by students, for students, with the intent of disrupting the classroom and really pushing the envelope on the contemporary education system. This is the largest student run hackathon encompassing all the major San Francisco Bay Area Universities and those along the west coast.

DeeMe

DeeMe is a new way to instant message. It lets users combine amazing typography, allowing them to chat using more personalized, beautiful messages that they can hold on to. From its 3rd week of release, DeeMe was featured on ProductHunt and Mashable. I worked with CEO Frode Alver Nilsen as UC Berkeley's Student Ambassador and provided feeback on the app.

Ava

Ava is the fastest and easiest way for deaf and hard-of-hearing people to understand and participate in conversations with people who can hear. Ava aims to transform communications for the deaf and hard-of-hearing people by bridging communication gaps with a 24/7 personal, lightning fast, and affordable captioning experience. I was briefly involved with this startup for Social Media.
