About Me

This is where I introduce myself.

  • ⇓

First impressions last, which is why I always strive to make mine phenomenal.
Please help me help you to get to know me a bit better.
All I ask from you is an open-mind.

I'm currently a third year who has the wonderful privilege of studying Computer Science at UC Berkeley. Even just a year ago, I would have never believed -- or even thought -- that I would end up coding my way to a career; prior to my first Computer Science class at UC Berkeley, I had never written a single line of code. After numerous ventures to explore my passions in an attempt to decide my undergraduate major, I took my very first steps into the Computer Science world. I ultimately continued to pursue it because I realized its powerful potential to contribute to the bigger picture on a global scale. My life aspiration is to utilize my knowledge in Computer Science to help mobilize nonprofit organizations and start my own nonprofit as well someday.
